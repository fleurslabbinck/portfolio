About

This project was a Bomberman remake that was part of an exam assignment for a course at DAE, together with my 2D Engine. The purpose of the project was to test the abilities of my engine and proof my grasp of the used design patterns.

What I Learned

Making this project was a huge learning experience. It taught me more than just understanding and implementing the design patterns used in the engine. Some mechanics were way more complicated than I initially imagined, and required some challenging problem-soling skills. Overall, this was a challenging, but most of all fun project to work on.
