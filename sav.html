Shield And Veil

Project Info
  • Team size: 4
  • Time frame: 1 month (August, 2024)
  • Framework: Unreal Engine
  • Language: C++
About

Shield And Veil was created for a game designing course at DAE. Constrained by the keywords "Shield" and "Veil", I had to make a prototype in Unreal Engine. The concept I came up with was a 3rd persone action-adventure game. You play as a knight that has to follow a path through a forest, guarded by archers, to rescue the princess. The knight has multiple tools available: a sword, a shield and a veil. With the sword, you can kill an archer in close range. The shield allows the knight to block incoming arrows. The shield and veil however also exhibit a special ability: when standing in a ray of sunshine that is peaking through the foliage, the knight can reflect the ray and aim at guards to incinerate them, and the veil serves as a cload of invisibility.

What I learned

I had a very good time making this project, putting everything I had previously learned while working with Unreal Engine to good use, and refining my skills. I really enjoyed implementing the mechanic that reflects the ray of sunshine, and working on the enemy behavior was the summit of the project.
